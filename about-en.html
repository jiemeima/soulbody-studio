About Us

We bring textile intelligence to the surface of robots.

SOULBODY STUDIO

Redefining Robot Apparel Through Knitting

SOULBODY STUDIO is a robot-apparel studio centered on knitted bionic soft-skin technology. We believe the surface that allows robots to share human spaces should not be limited to cold metal or rigid plastic. It can be flexible, conformal, removable, washable and capable of supporting tactile functions.

Our process combines knitted loop structures, 3D knit engineering and integral shaping. This allows a soft shell to follow joint movement, cover complex geometry and preserve access to sensors, charging ports and thermal zones.

Our work spans humanoid and embodied robots, medical rehabilitation systems, dexterous hands, exhibitions and branded robot applications.

Approach

Textile Thinking, Engineering Discipline

Geometry First

Every project starts with the robot body, motion range and operating boundaries.

Material by Function

Yarns and structures are chosen for elasticity, ventilation, durability, care and optional sensing.

Prototype and Validate

Fit, motion and maintenance are tested before a process is released for production.

Flexible Manufacturing

Rapid samples and small-to-medium batches support the fast iteration cycles of robotics teams.

Vision

A More Functional and Approachable Robot Surface

We want soft robotics surfaces to become an engineered product category: visually coherent, serviceable and ready for future sensing.
