Humanoid Robot Solutions

Functional soft covers developed around geometry, motion and real operating conditions.

Challenges

The Robot Body Is Not a Standard Mannequin

Every platform has different joints, thermal zones, sensors, service openings and brand language.

Complex Geometry

Non-standard surfaces require engineered shaping rather than conventional garment grading.

High-Motion Joints

Shoulders, elbows, hips and knees need controlled extension without bunching or migration.

Thermal & Sensor Boundaries

Ventilation and precise openings keep cooling, vision, audio and ranging systems unobstructed.

Maintenance

Modules must be removable and replaceable for testing, repair and frequent platform iteration.
